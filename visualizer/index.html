Map randomizer info

  • Select a step in the top-left corner to focus on areas that are logically reachable on that step. One-way-reachable areas are darker.
  • Select an item on the map to show a path to reach it (white) and return to the Ship (yellow).
  • Click on the Mother Brain Room to show the intended escape route.
  • Double-click a room to pull up the Wiki page for that room.
  • You can also modify the items to create a plando.
