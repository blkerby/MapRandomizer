Fast baby cutscene

If enabled, the delay in the cutscene is reduced before the Baby enters and drains the Sidehopper. If it attaches to Samus, it will more quickly release from Samus after the drain is complete.

This setting does not affect the movement of the Baby during the phase where Samus can perform the Baby Skip, nor does it affect the rate at which Samus' energy is drained if the Baby attaches to Samus.
