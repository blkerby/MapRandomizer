Enemy drops are buffed

If enabled, then certain enemy drops are improved:

  • Small energy drops give 10 energy.
  • Power Bomb drops give 2 Power Bombs.
  • Respawning enemies drop Power Bombs at double the normal rate. This affects Gamets, Zebs, Geegas, Zebbos, Zoas, and Coverns.
  • Zebs, Geegas, and Kagos drop Supers at double the normal rate.

Details

The increased Power Bomb and Super drop probabilities come out of the Nothing, Missile, and Small Energy drop probabilities. Large Energy drop rates are increased to compensate for the loss of some Small Energy drop probability. The doubled Power Bomb drop probabilities combines with the increased drop value (each Power Bomb drop giving 2 Power Bombs) to result in an overall quadrupled rate of Power Bomb farming.
