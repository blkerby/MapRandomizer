Hazard markers

If enabled, hazard markers (yellow & black stripes) will be placed on certain doors and elevators that have a high chance of death or a softlock on the other side. This includes the entrances to the following locations, either directly or via the Toilet:

  • Mt. Everest top door
  • Oasis top door
  • Fish Tank top left door
  • Fish Tank top right door
  • Yoink Room left and right door
  • Amphitheatre left door
  • Tourian Hopper Room left door
  • Big Pink crumble block left door
  • Ice Beam Gate Room crumble block top left door
  • Mickey Mouse Room left door
  • Fast Pillars Setup Room top right door
  • Ridley's Room top right door
  • Main Hall elevator

If the "Collectible" wall jump option is enabled, then the entrances to following two locations also have hazard markers:

  • West Ocean Gravity Suit door
  • Crocomire's Room top door
