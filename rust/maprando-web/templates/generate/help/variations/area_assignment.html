Area assignment

Each map is partitioned into six connected regions, which are assigned to in-game areas in different ways depending on this setting:

  • Standard: Landing Site is always in Crateria. Mother Brain Room is always in Tourian. The other areas are assigned based on their size, from largest to smallest: Norfair, Brinstar, Maridia, Wrecked Ship.

  • Size: The areas are assigned based on their size (in number of map tiles), from largest to smallest: Norfair, Brinstar, Maridia, Crateria, Tourian, Wrecked Ship.

  • Depth: The areas are assigned based on their highest map tile, from highest to lowest: Crateria, Wrecked Ship, Tourian, Brinstar, Maridia, Norfair.

  • Random: The areas are assigned randomly.

Additional options are available by clicking the gear button on the right.
