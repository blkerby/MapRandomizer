Speed Booster

This affects the Speed Booster item.

  • Vanilla: Speedbooster is a single collectible item and functions the same as the vanilla game.
  • Split: Vanilla Speed Booster is replaced with two new collectible items, "Spark Booster" and "Blue Booster".

This setting is taken into account in the logic.

Blue Booster will allow Samus to retain blue speed and perform tricks like blue Space Jumps, Blue Springball bounces, blue speed balls and Temporary blue chains but not shinespark.

Spark Booster will allow samus to shinespark, spikesuit (if not disabled) but without the ability to perform any of the blue speed abilities that Blue Booster enables.
