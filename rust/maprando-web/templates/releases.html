  • Preset is added for "Community Race Season 4".

    • This allows finer-grained control over the tiles revealed, e.g. maps, saves, objectives.

    • This is "Forced" but with prioritizing placing key items in the same area as the last step.

Version 118 (2025-06-13):

  • New tile themes "Blue Brinstar" and "Invisible" are available.
  • Fireflea rooms are adjusted across tile themes to improve visibility.
  • Customization of the amount and types of objectives is now available.

    • This can be disabled, e.g. if wanting Random objectives to be a surprise.

    • With this, E-Tanks refill reserve energy as well as regular energy when collected.
  • New quality-of-life option "Energy stations refill reserves" is added (High).
    • When enabled, energy stations refill reserve energy in addition to regular energy.
  • New quality-of-life option "Disableable E-Tanks" is added (High).
    • When enabled, E-Tanks may be disabled in the equipment screen by pressing Select + X, and re-enabled by pressing Select + Y.
  • New quality-of-life option "Reserve energy backward transfer" is added (Max).
    • When enabled, energy may be transferred to reserve tanks by setting reserves to Manual in the pause equipment screen and holding B + Up.
  • New quality-of-life option "Fanfares" is added, for enabling vanilla item fanfares.
  • Leniency settings are added for Crystal Flash clips and X-mode (under "Skill assumptions").
  • A new option "Custom start location" is added.
  • Mother Brain 1 now requires all objectives to be complete before the cutscene will trigger.
  • On the spoiler map, hovering over a step now shows additional collectible tanks and ammo.

Version 116 (2024-12-16):

  • New item progression preset "Technical" is added:
    • This is like "Challenge", but tanks and Wave Beam are placed as filler.
  • New tile theme "Pink Brinstar" is available.
  • New map pools for both Tame and Wild maps:
    • The size of the pools is increased (to 38763 Tame maps and 52077 Wild maps).
    • There is much less bias for Phantoon to connect to large rooms (e.g. West Ocean).
    • On Tame maps, West Ocean is much more likely to have a 2-tile room in the Bowling Alley Path space.
    • Room connections may show various other differences in their patterns.
    • Save station balancing is slightly improved.
  • Fixed an issue where Early filler items were not treated as filler after the first placement (a bug introduced in v115, hotfixed 2024-12-03).
    • This affected ETanks on Normal and Tricky item progression settings.
  • Early filler item placement is limited to two-way reachable locations.
    • On Normal and Tricky item progression, this means an early ETank will more consistently be placed somewhere that it is possible to collect and get out with it.
    • If an early filler item is placed in a one-way reachable location as a regular filler item and then becomes two-way reachable before the item is placed elsewhere as early filler, this will count as the early filler item instead of placing another.
  • Hazard markers are added to the door leading to the top-right of Fast Pillars Setup Room.
  • The "Refill energy for escape" option now refills reserves in addition to regular energy.
  • The "Enemies cleared during escape" option now retains some more harmless enemies:
    • Komas: the Blue Brinstar face statues
    • Kamers: the moving platforms in Double Chamber and other rooms
  • After defeating the first phase of Mother Brain, the left doorway becomes solid (in case it was open by entering from the left).
  • Improvements to spoiler map:
    • The map initially shows "Step 1" and centers on the initial accessible region.
    • Starting location is shown by a Samus helmet icon. Clicking it shows a logical route from the starting location to the hub and back.
    • Bosses, minibosses, and other game flags are shown by icons. Clicking an icon shows a logical route to obtain the flag.
    • Logically collectible flags are listed in the side panel when viewing step details.
    • The Ship is indicated by an icon. Clicking it shows a logical route for the escape.
    • Icon types can be toggled on/off, using the settings button in the top-right.
  • Fixes to "vanilla" bugs:
    • Leaving Kraid Room during the boss fight caused graphical corruption of the HUD.
    • Leaving a room with too many sprites on screen would crash the game (e.g. in Shaktool Room or Kraid Room).
    • A closing door animation could sometimes spawn in the wrong Y position.
  • The frequency of SRAM writing is reduced, resolving an issue where save data would be lost on certain platforms.
  • The unusual vanilla behavior of the door when entering the top of Etecoon Shaft is partially restored:
    • The door closing animation occurs off-screen, causing the door to be immediately closed on entry.
  • Logic updates:
    • New notable strats:
